Privacy and Static Site Boundaries

static-site privacy/CDN logs/external-link boundaries

This privacy page explains the boundaries of a simple static website. The pages are plain HTML, CSS, and image files hosted through a third-party static provider, so normal server or CDN logs may record basic request details.

Those logs can include IP address, browser user agent, requested URL, status code, and time of access. They are used by hosting infrastructure for delivery, abuse prevention, diagnostics, and caching rather than for a visitor account system.

This article cluster does not ask visitors to create an account, submit payment information, or upload documents. External links may lead to sites with their own privacy policies, cookies, analytics, affiliate disclosures, and checkout flows.

If you follow a product or reference link, review that destination separately. This page only describes the static pages in this cluster and the ordinary hosting boundary around them.
